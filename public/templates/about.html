About

What is this?

This page was created with the purpose of extracting all the songs of an artist from any playlist, thus creating an exclusive playlist for the artist.Initially, it was made as a practice project, but I liked the result so much that I decided to style the page and add new functionalities

Here is the special feature

Features

The special feature mentioned earlier allows you to add songs by an artist by extracting them from any playlist, creating an exclusive playlist for that artist on your profile.

Additionally, you can view the top 10 artists and songs you have listened to the most within a time range of 1 month to 1 year (or more).

How to Use?

First, you need to:

  • -log in with your Spotify account. Don't worry, we won't steal your information as we use Spotify's service directly-
  • -After logging in, you can explore options like viewing your statistics or creating a playlist for your favorite artist-
